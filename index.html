Understanding Bedwetting

Bedwetting (nocturnal enuresis) is involuntary urination while asleep. It's common and often improves with time and management strategies.

Common Causes

  • Small bladder capacity
  • Deep sleep patterns
  • Hormonal factors
  • Stress or anxiety
  • Genetic factors
  • Medical conditions

Management Tips

  • Limit fluids 2-3 hours before bed
  • Empty bladder before sleeping
  • Establish bedtime routines
  • Use positive reinforcement
  • Track patterns to identify triggers
  • Consider bedwetting alarms

When to Seek Help

Consult a healthcare provider if:

  • Bedwetting starts suddenly after dry period
  • Accompanied by pain or discomfort
  • Occurring after age 7
  • Affecting daily life significantly
